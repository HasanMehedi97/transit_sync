Project Architecture Notes
--------------------------
1. A simulated public transport vehicle generates operational data.
2. The generated data includes GPS tracking, ticketing, and fuel/engine telemetry.
3. When connectivity is unavailable, event files are stored locally in the pending folder.
4. When connectivity becomes available, pending files are uploaded to Azure Blob Storage.
5. After successful upload, files are moved to the synced folder.
6. Each uploaded event is enriched with syncedAt and syncDelaySeconds fields.
7. Operational alerts such as HIGH_ENGINE_TEMP and LOW_FUEL are logged locally.
8. A summary script provides counts of pending files, synced files, alerts, and delayed synchronizations.
9. The architecture demonstrates a resilient store-and-forward cloud ingestion model.
